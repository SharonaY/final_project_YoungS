Accessibility doesn't have to be a struggle

Central Florida offers a wide range of attractions, parks,restaurants, and more. Living with a disability does not mean you have to miss out!

Don't let limited mobility be a barrier to getting out and enjoying the activities you love. Many activities are ADA compliant and my offer accessibility accomodations to fit your needs.

Gatorland

Gatorland has an accessible zip line experience! This attraction is accessible to people with mobility challenges or other disabilities. The ride, which is wheelchair accessible, allows participants to travel 350 feet down a zip line, soaring over the park and a lake of giant alligators
